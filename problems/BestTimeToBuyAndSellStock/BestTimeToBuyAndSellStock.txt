Say you have an array for which the ith element is the price of a given stock on day i.

Design an algorithm to find the maximum profit. You can only make one transaction. You may not engage in multiple transactions at the same time (ie, you must sell the stock before you buy again).

Print the max profit you earned.


the first line of input is the number of arrays and the following lines are arrays to be processed.
 
sample input:
2
1,2
6,1,3,2,4,7

sample output:
1
6
